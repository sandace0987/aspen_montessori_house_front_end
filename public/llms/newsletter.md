# The Aspen Leaf — Newsletter

**URL:** https://aspenmontessori.in/newsletter

The Aspen Leaf is the monthly newsletter of Aspen Montessori House, sharing news, events, student stories, and Montessori insights from our Hyderabad campus.

Each edition includes:
- Highlights from the classrooms
- Upcoming events and celebrations
- Tips for parents on the Montessori approach at home
- Student achievements and milestones

For previous editions or to subscribe, contact aspenmontessorihouse@gmail.com.
