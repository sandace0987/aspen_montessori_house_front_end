# Gallery — Aspen Montessori House

**URL:** https://aspenmontessori.in/gallery

Photos of life at Aspen Montessori — classrooms, outdoor play, art activities, celebrations, and happy children learning through Montessori methods in Hyderabad.

The gallery captures:
- Prepared Montessori classrooms with authentic materials
- Children working independently and in mixed-age groups
- Outdoor play and nature-based learning
- Festivals, performances, and community events
- Sports day (Khel Aspen) and graduation ceremonies
