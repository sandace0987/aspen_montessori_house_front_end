# Programs — Aspen Montessori House

**URL:** https://aspenmontessori.in/programs

Each program follows authentic Montessori pedagogy tailored to your child's developmental stage, in a carefully prepared mixed-age environment.

## Toddlers (1.5–2.5 years)

**Timing:** 9:00 AM – 12:30 PM

Sensory exploration and early independence in a safe, nurturing environment. Focus on motor skills, language emergence, and gentle social interactions.

## Pre-Primary (3–5+ years)

**Timing:** Montessori-1 & Montessori-2 (Shift 1): 9:00 AM – 12:30 PM, (Shift 2): 11:30 AM – 3:00 PM | Montessori-3: 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM (Extended Hours)

Building foundations through hands-on Montessori materials — from early exploration to school readiness. Children progress through Practical Life, Sensorial, Arithmetic, Language, and Cultural activities.

## Daycare (1.5–6 years)

**Timing:** 9:00 AM – 5:30 PM

Extended care that blends Montessori activities with rest, meals, and structured play. Ideal for working parents who want continuity of the Montessori approach throughout the day.

## Enrichment Activities

Music, dance, art & craft, storytelling, outdoor play, yoga, and seasonal celebrations — woven into the weekly rhythm of the school.
